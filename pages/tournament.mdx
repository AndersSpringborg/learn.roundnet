# Tournaments

## Uniform
talk to your partner about what to wear, and coordinate so you're wearing the same thing. Like a red short and black shorts. Is just for fun, you don't have to.

## In your bag
- Snacks
- Water
- Blanket to sit on
- Warm clothes, if it's cold
- Tripod
- Battery pack
- Bandages (Blister bandages, gauze, tape, etc.)
- Sports tape
- Sunscreen
- Sun glasses / Hat
